SolutionNet

Welcome to SolutionNet, a site devoted to sharing and comparing solutions for the puzzle game SpaceChem by Zachtronics Industries.

Registered users can upload their SpaceChem save file to have all of their solutions displayed and ranked against other uploaded solutions. It is also possible to view the same statistics used to generate the in-game graphs, but with specific numbers that can't be seen in-game.
